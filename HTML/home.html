Menu

Item Description

Chicken Caesar Salad

 A classic salad that combines grilled chicken, romaine lettuce, croutons, Parmesan cheese, and Caesar dressing. It's a healthy and filling option that's easy to prepare and customizable.

Vegetable Stir-Fry

 A colorful and nutritious dish that includes a mix of vegetables such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas, stir-fried with tofu or chicken and served over rice or noodles. It's a vegetarian-friendly option that can be made with a variety of sauces to suit different tastes.

BBQ Pulled Pork Sandwich

 A hearty sandwich made with slow-cooked, shredded pork in barbecue sauce, served on a bun with coleslaw. It's a comforting and satisfying choice that's perfect for a casual meal.
